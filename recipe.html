Recipe: Vegetable Burger


Cooking Time:30 min Origin: American

Delicious,lip-smacking and crunchy these words will only remind you of
this amazing American dish popularly kniwn as Burger. Now you can
make this delicacy at home.We are sharing with you the recipe for this dish
here with you

Ingredients


  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 2 slices cheese slices
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon refined oil
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon breadcrumbs
  • ginger paste as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 handful coriander leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 2 leaves lettuce loose-leaf

    • How to make Vegetable Burger


      Step 1 / 9
      Pressure cook carrots, peas and corn To make the burger patty, pressure cook the carrot, peas and sweet
      corn for 1 whistle or until soft. veggies

      Step 2 / 9
      Add the spices Add the cooked vegetables, chopped onions, red chilli powder, lemon juice, garam masala powder, salt and ginger-garlic paste to a large bowl.

      Step 3 / 9
      Add mashed potatoes Add lemon juice and mashed potatoes in the bowl, mix well until evenly combined. Shape the mixture into small/medium patties.

      Step 4 / 9
      Shallow fry the patties Now heat oil in a pan over medium flame. Roll the prepared patties in the breadcrumbs and shallow fry until golden brown on both sides.

      Step 5 / 9
      Keep aside Remove and keep aside.

      Step 6 / 9
      Prepare the burger Take one half of the burger bun. Spread some butter and place the lettuce on top.

      Step 7 / 9
      Add slices onions, cucumber and tomatoes Place the prepared vegetable patty on top. Top up with slices of onion, tomato, cucumber and cheese. If you want to make it even more delicious and healthy replace cheese with eggless mayonnaise.

      Step 8 / 9
      Burger is ready Cover it with the other half of the burger bun. If desired, add some ketchup on top. Secure it with a toothpick if desired.

      Step 9 / 9
      Serve! Serve at once with french fries. Make sure you like this recipe, rate it and let us know in the comments how it turned out to be.
